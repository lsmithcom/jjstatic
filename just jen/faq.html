How do I care for garments?

All garments may be machine washed in the gentle cycle, please turn the garment inside out. Like colors should be laundered together. Zippers from other garments washed may pull and snag the delicate items. Garments may shrink substantially in hot water. It is best to line dry the items to avoid shrinkage.
