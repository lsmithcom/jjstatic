• Start a Shop

    The process for selling apparel with your logo or design on our site is easy and fast. Check out the steps below and then click the Get Started button to begin the process.

    • Step 1

      Apply for a Shop

      The first step can be completed in less than 5 minutes. Just click on the Get Started button and answer a few quick questions and you are on your way.

    • Step 2

      Get a Quote on Your Custom Design

      The next step is to get a quote on your custom design (if you have not already done this). Go to the Custom section and click on the Get Quote button and upload your graphic, or describe the lettering and we can give you a quote.

    • Step 3

      Launch Your Store and Take Orders

      Once you are approved for a shop and have received a custom quote on your designs, we will build the product listings and then send you a link to the completed shop.

  • Step 1 of 4

    Will your shop be visible to everyone?

    • Public Shop

      A public shop is visible to everyone visiting our site and anyone can order from a public shop. Most public shops are drop-ship.

    • Private Shop

      A private shop allows you to restrict who can shop and buy your products. You privode a user name and pasword to your customers to access a private shop. Private shops are also best for group orders.

  • Step 2 of 4

    How Will Customers Order and Ship?

    • Drop Ship

      Drop ship orders are ordered individually, paid for and shipped directly to the customer. Each order is produced and processed separately, so they arrive based on the date ordered. Since it is less efficient, the cost of drop ship orders is higher, and shipping costs are generally higher.

    • Group Ship

      Your customers select the styles/colors/sizes they want and pay individually, but the order is produced together and shipped to one location. You set the ordering deadline for the group shipment.

    • Group Pay

      Your group members select the styles/colors/sizes they want, but there is one payment and shipment for the order. This is the easy way to collect sizes & colors, but get full wholesale pricing. You set the ordering deadline for the group shipment.
