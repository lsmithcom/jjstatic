    Start a Shop

  • Simplify Ordering

    Stop taking orders! Use our site to collect orders. Each member of your group or team can select sizes, colors and garment styles online, at their convenience.

  • Group or Drop Shipping

    We can drop ship orders directly to your customers, or set an order deadline and ship to one location. Save money on shipping with a group shipment.

  • Flexible Payment Options

    Customers can pay directly for their orders online or you can collect payment and pay as a group. Set your sales price and we will remit to you the mark-up.

  • Share with Your Group

    Send a link to your shop with your group, or to potential customers. They can visit the shop at their convenience and order products.
