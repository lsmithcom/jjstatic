Uniform Embellishment Options

  • Logos & Designs

    We can turn your logo or design into a rhinestone applique and apply it to your uniforms. Whether you choose a large complex embellishment or a smaller accent to your existing design, extra bling gets your team more attention.

  • Bling Patterns

    Embellish your uniforms with a variety of pattern options, from simple scatter-plots and star patterns to more unique patterns that match your uniform design. Work with us to plan out an embellishment strategy for your uniforms, practice leotards and spiritwear.

  • Bling Accents

    Bling accents can be used on many parts of a uniform, from simple accents around the neck, arms, legs or body to more complex accents on design elements of the garment. Crystals can be embellished on most areas of any garment, but there are some limitations when embellishing on printed areas and fabrics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Uniforms Shipping
Can you embellish my uniforms?

Each embellishment job for uniforms is unique, so it depends upon what you want embellished and the fabric. Sometimes we will recommend several options, to reduce the cost or make a project more efficient or improve on a design idea. You can always send us a message or call us to discuss with a live person.

Can you create my logo or design?

We can translate logos or designs that you provide into a rhinestone pattern, however, we do not design logos from scratch (you need a designer to do that). Embellishment of uniforms can include logos and patterns or highlighting of specific parts of a garment. If you need to talk to a human, feel free to call us at 310-539-6000.

How fast can I get my order?

Embellishment of uniforms can vary widely in time and labor, so it is impossible to generalize without specific information. There are some jobs that can be done within a few days and some that can take several weeks to complete. Since we are located in the United States, we do not have extended overseas shipping times.

What are the US shipping rates and options?

For wholesale orders, we get shipping quotes based on the weight and size dimensions of the package. We can estimate your shipping costs, but the exact shipping price will be calculated once we have your order complete and get an exact weight.

How fast can I get my order?

For wholesale orders, including embellishment of uniforms, the production of the order is dependent upon order quantity and complexity of the application. Each order is different, once we see what you need, we can provide a firm estimate of timing.

Do you offer free shipping?

Not on wholesale orders. The wholesale price is a great deal, so shipping charges are calculated separately.

How do I track my package?

When we ship your order an email is automatically sent you to with the tracking information. For packages shipped by Economy and Standard methods, go to usps.com and enter the tracking code. For packages shipped by 2-Day or Overnight Guaranteed, go to Fedex.com.
