How are the garments sized?

The sizing for each garment is unique, so we recommend that you check the size charts carefully to order the best size. We provide equivalent sizes and measurements for each garment. In general, our fitted garment styles run small, similar to junior sizes, and our unisex styles run large. If you still have questions, you can call us and we can provide more clarification.

How do I care for garments?

All garments may be machine washed in the gentle cycle, please turn the garment inside out. Like colors should be laundered together. Zippers from other garments washed may pull and snag the delicate items. Garments may shrink substantially in hot water. It is best to line dry the items to avoid shrinkage.

How fast can I get my order?

All orders placed before 4 PST will ship the following business day or we will contact you. This includes personalized designs with names. Time to reach you is determined by the shipping method you select. Please contact us for production times on wholesale orders or custom logo designs.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, on retail orders more than $100 we offer Free Economy shipping (wholesale orders pay shipping). Enter code FREESHIP in the promotion code field during checkout. Free Shipping is always sent by Economy delivery. Free Shipping only applies to orders shipping within the United States.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to Canada, Europe, Australia and Japan. Select Global Shipping for all international shipments. If you do not select the correct shipping method for international shipments, your order will be held until we can reach you regarding additional shipping charges. Get more shipping rates.

How do I track my package?

When we ship your order an email is automatically sent you to with the tracking information. For packages shipped by Economy and Standard methods, go to usps.com and enter the tracking code. For packages shipped by 2-Day or Overnight Guaranteed, go to Fedex.com.
