Custom Bikini FAQ

Custom Styles & Sizes Shipping
Can you create my logo or design on a bikini?

We can translate your logo or design into a rhinestone or printed design, however, we do not create logos or designs from scratch. Remember, there is a limited amount of space on a bikini, so some designs may not be possible, or will need to be re-worked.

How do I get started on a custom bikini design?

Simple designs can be entered into the form online, but for more complicated designs, it is best to contact us and discuss how your design can work on a bikini.

What bikini styles are available?

We offer only one style of bikini top and two styles of bikini bottoms - tie-side and euro bottoms. All of our bikinis are manufactured by Chica Rica and are a popular style and fabric, available in several colors. Please begin the customization process to see more information on the bikini styles.

How are the bikinis sized?

Size and style information on the bikinis is available from the "Styles & Sizes" link in the footer of every page on the website. Generally, the tie-side styles are more forgiving on size, as the tie-side can be adjusted to fit a wide size range. Bikini tops can be more difficult to size, so contact us if you have questions.

How fast can I get my order?

All orders placed before 4 PST will ship the following business day or we will contact you. This includes personalized designs with names. Time to reach you is determined by the shipping method you select. Please contact us for production times on wholesale orders or custom logo designs.

What are the US shipping rates and options?

Please check our shipping rate section for all services and prices.

Do you offer free shipping?

Unfortunately, at this time there is no free shipping option that is always available, but we do run special promotions that will sometimes include free shipping.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to Canada, Europe, Australia and Japan. Select Global Shipping for all international shipments. If you do not select the correct shipping method for international shipments, your order will be held until we can reach you regarding additional shipping charges. Get more shipping rates.

How do I track my package?

When we ship your order an email is automatically sent you to with the tracking information. For packages shipped by Economy and Standard methods, go to usps.com and enter the tracking code. For packages shipped by 2-Day or Overnight Guaranteed, go to Fedex.com.
